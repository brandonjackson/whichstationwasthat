Station Identification Panel

(No. 68.)

STAMBOUL

(Constantinople, Turkey).

Wavelength: 1,200 m. Power: 7 kw.

Approximate distance from London: 1,580 m.

Call (in Turkish): Allo! Telsiz-telegrafie Istamboul; in French: Ici, Radiophonie de Stamboul.

No interval signal.

Closes down with a few bars of Turkish National Anthem and final “Goodnight” in Turkish, French, German and English.
