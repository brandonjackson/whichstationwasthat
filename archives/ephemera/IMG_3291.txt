WORLD-RADIO Station Identification Panel (No. 91) CARTHAGE (Tunis) Wavelength : 1850 m. Frequency : 154 kc. Power : 2 kw. Approximate Distance from London : 1,130 miles. Call : “ Ici Poste de Radiodiffusion de Tunis.” Opening Signal : Carillon of bells. Interval Call : “ Ici Radio Carthage.” Male Announcer. No Interval Signal. Times of Transmission : Broadcasts from 6 p.m. daily; Main Evening Concert at 8.30 p.m. Dance Transmissions : Sundays, Wednesdays, and Saturdays to 11 p.m. G.M.T. Closes down with “ La Marseillaise ” and usual French formula. (Copyright) A booklet containing a large number of these panels can be obtained of B.B.C. Publications, Savoy Hill, W.C. Price 1/-.
