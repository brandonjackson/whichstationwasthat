Northerner: At 10.30, I heard 2FC at Sydney, on 603 metres, carried through a relay at 2BL.

Northerner: There were other relays at KDKA and WGY which were also relaying 2FC, the first on 947 metres and the other on 379 metres, and indeed, there was a little interference.

Northerner: I have noticed 2BL at 462 metres which was good, but not up to 2FC, and I am inclined to think the two stations, KDKA and WGY, were not relaying on that particular occasion.

Northerner: 2FC is the more powerful of the two stations, and it was evident that WGY was relaying a 2FC programme, not 2BL, but I could not get the call-signs, and in each case 2FC was better.

Northerner: I am inclined to think it is 4YA at Dunedin which I heard, not 3YA at Christchurch, as the frequency was so much closer to that of 2FC, and I do not think there is any doubt that it was 4YA which I heard.

Northerner: The Australian programmes are very good, with excellent music, and at 10.45 I heard 2FC again which must have come up in strength.

Northerner: Returning to 2FC, I have heard it work evidently on 2000 miles and very much farther than that, that is to say, in England and round about.

Northerner: For some reason, however, there are only a few nights in each month in which they can get through, and this I have found by careful logging every night.

Northerner: 6CB has been received for two years at such times, and generally weaker than 2FC and 4YA, but at times as strong as the last named.

Northerner: I have heard 6CB at Tasmania several times, and on one occasion they were relaying the 2FC broadcast of a Test Match and talking a good deal, and it was possibly 4YA relaying 6CB.

Northerner: In this way, I suppose, they hoped to get a complete reception over the whole of the Commonwealth.

Northerner: 6CB, therefore, was evidently relaying, in the early days, 2FC, or if not, 2BL, and the time I heard them, they were relaying the KDKA relay of 2FC.

Northerner: I have recently heard 2FC relayed from KFI on a slightly shorter wavelength, of roughly 650 metres; DF's at KFI (Los Angeles) were very good, with others of the same nature, but I am quite sure there were no local stations on at the time to interfere.

Northerner: WJZ is not such a good relayer of 2FC, but is usually excellent, relaying WEAF in the same way.
