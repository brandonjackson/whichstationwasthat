WORLD-RADIO Station identification panel

TOULOUSE (France).

Wavelength: 389.6 m. Power: 2 kw.

Approximate distance from London: 550 m.

Call: “Ici Radio Toulouse, Emissions de la Radio Toulouse.”

Interval Signal: Ticking of metronome.

Abbreviated Call: “Ici Radio Toulouse,” repeated after each item in a programme. A lady announcer at times. Closes down after bars of “La Marseillaise,” preceded by a local patriotic March, “La Toulousaine.”
