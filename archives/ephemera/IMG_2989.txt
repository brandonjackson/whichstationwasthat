WORLD-RADIO  
Station Identification Panel  

LENINGRAD (Russia).  
Wavelength: 1,000 m. Power: 10 kw.  
Approximate distance from London: 1,350 miles.  
Opening Signal: Gong or chimes.  
Call: “Allo! Allo! Allo! Radio Peredacha, Leningrad.”  
No interval signal.  
Male and female announcers.  
Frequently relays opera and also transmissions from Moscow (RDW).  
Closes down with strokes on gong to indicate time and a few bars of the “International.”
