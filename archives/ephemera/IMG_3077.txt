Station Identification Panel

No. 80.

KDKA (East Pittsburgh, Pa.), U.S.A.

Approximate distance from London : 3,120 miles.

Wavelength : 315·6 m.   Frequency : 950 kc.   Power : 30 kw.

Opening Call : “This is KDKA, the Westinghouse Electric broadcasting station of Pittsburgh, calling.”

No interval signal, but “KDKA, East Pittsburgh,” repeated between items.

Slogan : “The Pioneer Broadcasting Station of the World,” used when closing down.

Relays of the studio programmes are also transmitted on 62 m. (4,839 kc.), 26 m. (11,194 kc.), and on 14 m. (21,429 kc.).
