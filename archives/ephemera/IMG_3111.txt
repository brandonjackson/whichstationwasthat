WORLD-RADIO Station Identification Panel (No. 82).

SCHEVENINGEN-HAVEN (Holland).

Wavelength: 1950 m. Frequency: 154 kc. Power: 2·5 kw.

Approximate Distance from London: 180 miles.

Opening Call: “Allo! hier de Zakelijke Omroep te Scheveningen-Haven.”

Male announcer. No musical broadcasts, but transmissions of commercial intelligence, Stock-Exchange and other quotations.

Throughout day at various times from 6.55 a.m. G.M.T. When news is given the bulletin is usually preceded by the call: “Hier Persbureau Vaz Dias te Amsterdam” (the name of the agency responsible for the transmission).
