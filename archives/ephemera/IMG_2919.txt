WORLD-RADIO Station Identification Panel BILBAO (Spain), EAJ 9. No. 57. Wavelength: 436 m. (434·8 m.). Power: 1 kw. Approximate distance from London: 570 miles. Call: “Atención! Esta es la Estación Union Radio de Vizcaya, Emisora Radio Carlton.” No interval signal, but abbreviated call repeated between items. On some evenings relays transmissions from Madrid (EAJ7). Closes down with: “Buenas noches a todos” (“Good-night, everybody”), followed by a few bars of the Spanish National Anthem.
