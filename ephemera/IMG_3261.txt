WORLD-RADIO Station Identification Panel

(No. 90) MELBOURNE, Australia (3LO, Short Wave)

Wavelength: 32 m. (as announced). Frequency: 9,375 kc. Power: 5 kw. Approximate distance from London: 10,000 miles. Times of transmission: Sundays, 6.30 p.m. to 8.30 p.m. (G.M.T.). Concert of gramophone records. At 8 p.m. a time signal (striking clock) from Malvern Observatory, Melbourne, followed by weather report.
