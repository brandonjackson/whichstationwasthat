WORLD-RADIO

Station Identification Panel

(No. 78).

WGY (Schenectady, New York, U.S.A.).

Approximate distance from London: 3,050 miles.

Wavelength: 379.5 m. Frequency: 790 kc. Power: 30 kw.

Opening Call: “This is WGY of the General Electric Company, Schenectady.”

No interval signal, but call letters repeated between items.

Experimental transmissions are also carried out under call sign 2XAF on 32·77 m. (9,155 kc.) on every Tuesday and Thursday at 11 p.m., and on Saturdays at 11.30 p.m. B.S.T. A relay of the WGY programmes on Sundays, Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays of each week is also transmitted on 22·02 m. (13,623 kc.).

Call letters: 2XAD.
