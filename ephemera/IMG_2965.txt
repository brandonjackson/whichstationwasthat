WORLD-RADIO Station Identification Panel (No. 62.)

GENEVA (Switzerland). Wavelength: 760 m. Power: 1.5 kw. Approximate distance from London: 468 miles. Opening Signal: A long, drawn-out whistle thrice repeated. Call: “Allo! Allo! Allo! Allo! Ici Radio Genève Auditorium de l’Hôtel de la Métropole.” No interval signal, but “Allo! Ici Radio Genève” repeated between items. Closing Sentence: “Bonsoir, mesdames,” etc. (Copyright.)

Letters should be addressed to The Editor, “WORLD-RADIO,” Savoy Hill, Strand, London, W.C.
