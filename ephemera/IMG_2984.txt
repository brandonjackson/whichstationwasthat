WORLD-RADIO Station Identification Panel (No. 65.)

CORK (Irish Free State). Wavelength: 400 m. Power: 1·5 kw. Approximate distance from London: 363 miles.

Call Sign: 6 C K.

Opening Signal: Tuning note.

Opening and Interval Call (in Irish and English): Irish: “Se Seo Corcaigh ag Glaohach” (Shay shuh Kur-kig egg Glay-ock). English: “Cork calling—6 C K.”

Relays Dublin. Transmits its own programme one night weekly from 8—10.30 p.m.
