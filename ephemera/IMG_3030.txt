LANGENBERG (Rhineland), Germany.  

(Revised.)  

Wavelength: 468·8 m.  

Frequency: 640 kc. Power: 25 kw.  

Approximate distance from London: 305 miles.  

Call: Achtung! Achtung! Hier Westdeutscher Sender Langenberg. Usually opens with Studio chimes (four bells).  

Interval Signal: U in morse ... — Relays entertainments from the Muenster, Dortmund, Düsseldorf, and Cologne Studios. Closes down with “Deutschland Lied” (“Deutschland über alles”).  

Letters should be addressed to The Editor, “WORLD-RADIO,” Savoy Hill, Strand, London, W.C.
