Station Identification Panel

HANOVER (Germany).

(No. 59).

Wavelength: 297 m. Power: 0·7 kw.

Approximate distance from London: 418 miles.

Call: “Hier Norddeutsche Sender Hanover” (if own transmission).

Intervals indicated by stroke on gong to denote minutes.

Morse Sign H.R. ( . . . — . ) sent out.

Closes down with usual German formula, to which is added, on occasions, the National Anthem.

For most transmissions relays Hamburg. Lady Announcer.
