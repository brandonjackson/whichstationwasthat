WORLD-RADIO Station Identification Panel (No. 60).

CRACOW (Poland).

Wavelength: 422 m. Power: 500 watts.

Approximate distance from London: 900 m. 160 m. S.S.W. of Warsaw.

Opening Signal: Three blasts on a trumpet.

Call: “Hallo! Hallo! Radio Craców (pron. Rah-dee-o Krakov), piers-ce ras stire dva cesza-dwa” (242 m.).

Both male and female announcers.

Abbreviated Call between Items: Radio Cracow.

Closes down with National Anthem, Dabrowski Mazurka.

Apart from own transmissions, relays Warsaw programmes.
