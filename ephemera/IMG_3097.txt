Station Identification Panel

(Revised.)

ROME (Italy), 1 RO.

Wavelength: 450 m. (666 kc.) Power: 3 kw.

Approximate distance from London: 890 m.

Preliminary Signal: A few chords played on harmonium.

Interval Signal: Two bells of different tone.

Call: “Unione Radiofonica Italiana, Roma, Uno air-o (1 RO).”

Closes down with: “Unione Radiofonica Italiana, Radio Roma. Fine della transmissione,” followed by the Italian National Anthem, “Marcia Reale,” and the words “Buono notte a tutti” (good-night, everybody).
